A poem

Two lost people in the night
looking for a lap to belong
collide into each other
The one says:
"I'm a rock
solid and stable
you can lean on me.
Years may wear away my surface
but I am and I stay."
The other hesitates.
"I am not solid."
But that's exactly why.
A strong one does not need one like themselves.
An insecure one cannot live with someone alike.
Have the courage to lean on.
Have the courage and you'll see.
You're stronger.
